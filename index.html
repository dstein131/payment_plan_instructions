Setting up a new Payment Plan

Property Selection

Fill in the appropriate property or sale ID into the Property Selection dialog box and click on the ‘Submit’ button. NOTE: When you begin typing, the system will start to display potential matches to the ID you are typing.

Payment Plan Details

The Payment Plan Details for the property you selected above will now be displayed. Verify this is the property you are seeking and continue to fill out the information in the details section to begin creating a payment plan. An example has been provided in the screenshot below with sensitive information redacted. NOTE: Fields with an asterisk * are required fields.

Down Payment

It is important to note that if the owner wishes to make a payment on the same day the payment plan is created, the Down Payment field will need to be used to record that payment. This will lower the monthly payments and keep the same number of payments.

Saving the Payment Plan

Once the plan details have been entered, the plan needs to be saved – select the ‘Save Plan’ button to save the plan. The resulting page will show additional options and the payment table. NOTE: You will be asked to confirm if you want to save the plan and if there are any missed fields in the form, you’ll see a message at the top of the page with details.

At this point, the plan has been saved with the details entered in the plan details. If all of this information is correct, the plan needs to be acknowledged and signed by the property owner. If an email or cell phone number was collected in the payment plan details, click on the ‘Activate Plan’ button to send an email/SMS confirmation to the email address or cell phone for validation. You may also click on ‘View Agreement’ to display a copy of the payment plan which can be printed and signed by the property owner and a representative for the county. Once the agreement has been executed, the plan can be scanned and saved back to the Tax Sale System by selecting ‘Save Signed Agreement’ after choosing a file. After this, at any time, you can go back and select ‘Download Pay Plan’ to download this signed copy of the agreement. NOTE: By completing this process, it does not update the status within the system for the parcel itself. Please refer to the section titled Updating Status within Tax Sale System if the county chooses to update the status once a payment plan is activated.
